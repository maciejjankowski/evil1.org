Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At evil1.org, privacy is not just a policy - it's fundamental to our mission. We expose corporate surveillance and data exploitation, so we take the protection of our users' privacy extremely seriously.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform. We are committed to transparency in our data practices and minimal data collection.

Information We Collect

We follow a strict principle of collecting only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Email addresses: Only for newsletter subscriptions and account communications
  • Anonymous usage data: Basic analytics to improve our platform (no personal identification)
  • Whistleblower submissions: Protected under attorney-client privilege and encrypted end-to-end
  • Account information: Only if you choose to create an account for premium features

We do NOT collect:

  • Social media data or browsing history
  • Location data or device information
  • Personal financial information
  • Third-party tracking data

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide the platform features you request
  • Communication: To send you updates about our mission and platform
  • Security: To protect our platform and users from abuse
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Platform improvement: To enhance our services based on aggregated, anonymized data

Data Security

We employ military-grade encryption and security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive communications
  • Zero-knowledge architecture for whistleblower submissions
  • Regular security audits by independent third parties
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Automatic data deletion policies for inactive accounts

Whistleblower Protection

For whistleblowers using our platform, we provide additional protections:

  • Anonymous submission options with no IP logging
  • Legal protection resources and attorney referrals
  • Secure document vault with bank-level encryption
  • No data retention beyond what is legally required
  • Emergency deletion protocols for sensitive information

Data Sharing

We NEVER sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations (with proper legal process)
  • To protect our rights in case of legal disputes
  • With service providers who help us operate (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (opt-out available)
  • No advertising cookies: We don't serve targeted advertising
  • No third-party tracking: We don't share data with advertisers
